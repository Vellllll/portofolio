About Me

Hi, I am Arvel Budiono, a backend developer with over 1 year of professional experience in a dynamic startup environment. I have contributed to developing and maintaining three key products at PT Buana Online Sejahtera: iBos (bus and travel ticketing system), SalesMania (sales management system), and VehiLoc (GPS tracking system). I've successfully released more than 70 features within one year, using mainly Python with the Flask framework, JavaScript for frontend integration, and PostgreSQL as the primary database.

Recently, I expanded my skills by working on a mini project with Golang for a sport field booking system and stepped into the role of Project Manager at CV IT PRO Solutions, overseeing planning, development, testing, and collaboration for SalesMania and VehiLoc.

I graduated in Computer Science from Soegijapranata Catholic University and participated in the Bangkit Academy 2023 cohort, focused on cloud computing and backend application development.
